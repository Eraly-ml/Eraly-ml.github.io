Projects & Publications

Projects

2025

KazBERT – BERT-based model fine-tuned for Kazakh language tasks. The model is trained using Masked Language Modeling (MLM) on a Kazakh, Russian, English text corpus. [repo]
Skin-AI – Skin-AI is a deep learning model based on ConvNeXt, optimized for skin disease classification using PyTorch Lightning. [repo]
YOLOv5 for Smoke Detection – This project is a real-time smoke detection system using a custom-trained YOLOv5 model. Upon detecting smoke, it automatically sends alerts — including annotated frames — to a specified Telegram chat. [repo]
Against Toxic Telegram – Against Toxic Telegram Bot is a Telegram bot designed to automatically analyze and delete toxic messages in groups and channels. [repo]
AI Telegram Doctor Detect – AI-driven Telegram bot for medical diagnostics. AI telegram bot for analyzing MRI images of the brain and helps to identify possible signs of cancer. [repo]
Central Asian Food Classification – Pre-trained ResNet-50 model fine-tuned on the Central Asian Food Dataset from ISSAI. [repo]
